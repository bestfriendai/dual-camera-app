# How to Access Advanced Controls

## Quick Guide

The Advanced Controls feature is now accessible in the DualLensPro app!

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Open the Camera App**
   - Launch DualLensPro

2. **Tap Settings Button**
   - Look for the gear icon (⚙️) in the top toolbar
   - Tap it to open Settings

3. **Find Advanced Section**
   - Scroll down in Settings
   - Look for the "ADVANCED" section
   - It's located between "FOCUS & EXPOSURE" and "APP SETTINGS"

4. **Tap Advanced Controls**
   - Tap the "Advanced Controls" button
   - Icon: slider.horizontal.3 (three horizontal sliders)
   - Settings will dismiss smoothly

5. **Use Advanced Controls**
   - The Advanced Controls panel will open
   - Adjust camera settings as needed
   - Tap the X button to close when done

---

## What's in Advanced Controls?

### Camera Selection
- Switch between Front and Back camera for individual control

### White Balance
- **Auto** - Automatic white balance
- **Sunny** - Optimized for bright sunlight
- **Cloudy** - Optimized for overcast conditions
- **Fluorescent** - Optimized for indoor fluorescent lighting
- **Incandescent** - Optimized for warm indoor lighting

### Video Stabilization
- **Auto** - Automatic stabilization selection
- **Standard** - Basic stabilization
- **Cinematic** - Smoothest footage (highest quality)
- **Off** - No stabilization

### Recording Quality
- **4K** - Highest quality (3840×2160)
- **1080p** - Full HD (1920×1080)
- **720p** - HD (1280×720)
- **480p** - SD (854×480)

### Exposure Compensation
- Slider to adjust exposure from -2 to +2
- Fine-tune brightness of your video

### Focus Lock
- Toggle to lock focus at current position
- Prevents automatic focus adjustments

## Troubleshooting

**Q: I don't see the Advanced Controls button**
- A: Make sure you're in the Settings screen (tap gear icon ⚙️)
- A: Scroll down to find the "ADVANCED" section

**Q: The panel doesn't open**
- A: Wait for Settings to dismiss completely (0.3 second delay)
- A: Try tapping the button again

**Q: Settings don't save**
- A: Changes are applied immediately to the camera
- A: Some settings may require camera restart to take effect

**Q: Some options are grayed out**
- A: Certain options may not be available on all devices
- A: Check if your device supports the feature (e.g., Center Stage)
